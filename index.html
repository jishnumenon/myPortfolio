Journey till Now.

NSS college of Engineering

I completed my Bachelors degree in Electronics and communication from the University of Calicut, NSS college of Engineering, by March 2014. I was an instructor at TecHub (a technical Hobby club) technical coordinator of Impulse 2k13 and 2k14 and a founding member of the Entrepreneurship Development Cell at NSS college of Engineering.

Perleybrook Labs

Perleybrook Labs LLC is a startup who does research and development on the cutting edge of cloud connected security systems among other things. Even though my official title was Hardware engineer, the startup environment there was supportive of pursuing any project of interest. So i did lot of offshoot prototypes which made me a jack of all trades.

University of Massachusetts, Lowell

My time at Perleybrook Labs opened my eyes to a whole new world of possibilities, I realised that I wanted to learn more about engineering and expand my horizon, So I joined the University of Massachusetts, lowell for MS in Computer Engineering. I will be graduating in May 2018.During my time here i was an active participant in the Difference Maker initiative. I and my team won prices in Prototyping , creative ventures and big Idea challenges.

Hansen Research Group

When I joined UML for my masters program, I really wanted to work on some exciting research with an interdisciplinary team. Thanks to Prof. Christopher Hansen, I got an opportunity to do this. Prof Hansen understood my passion and he gave me a chance to work in his research group at the Mechanical Engineering Department, even though my major was Computer Engineering. I worded on the latest 3D printing technology such as carbon fiber reinforced 3D printing and Ink write printing of Self Healing materials.
